Paradox!

A 2D puzzle-platformer where each level is played 2 times. In the first part you play as a young scientist, while in the second part you'll switch to an older one, who will try to help his younger self to reach the end of the level.

Pipeline Simulation

A 3D simulation of the creation process of assembled products in a factory through different machineries. During observation, time can be paused, accelerated, or decelerated, to check the events with different perspectives. Settings related to the machineries are also personalizable in real-time, keeping in mind the exploratory purpouse of the simulation.

SpotiQuiz

A project developed in Flutter with Dart language for smartphones and tablets, leveraging Spotify's API to let the users play quizzes tailored on their music preferences.

Github

Basket Mobile

A mobile game in which the player has to score as many points as possible in 60 seconds. Stopping the shot in the green zone means the player has performed a perfect shot, gaining more points. If the shot is stopped in the purple zone instead, the shot will touch the board, leading to extra points during certain timed events. In the challenge mode, you can compete against a bot.
